No-Noodle Zucchini Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Seasonings
  • Beef
  • Fresh produce
  • Frozen spinach
  • Tomato products
  • Wine
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Recipe instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Slice zucchini into thin strips, sprinkle lightly with salt, and let drain in a colander.
  3. Cook ground beef with black pepper in a skillet over medium-high heat until no longer pink. Add green pepper and onion; cook until softened.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, wine, basil, and oregano. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta, egg, and parsley.
  6. Layer half of the meat sauce, zucchini slices, ricotta mixture, spinach, mushrooms, and mozzarella in the pan. Repeat.
  7. Top with Parmesan, cover with foil, and bake at 325°F (165°C) for 45 minutes. Remove foil, bake an additional 15 minutes.
  8. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy! 🍽️👌
