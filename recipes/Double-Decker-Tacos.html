Double Decker Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet taco seasoning mix, divided
  • 1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
  • ⅔ cup water
  • 12 prepared crisp taco shells
  • 2 avocados
  • 1 tablespoon sour cream
  • 1 fresh lime, juiced
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 12 (7 inch) flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • ¼ red onion, chopped
  • ½ cup sour cream

Recipe instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat. Drain off excess grease.
  2. Mash the refried beans with the water in a small saucepan. Stir in the remaining taco seasoning. Heat over low heat until simmering.
  3. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Place the taco shells on a baking sheet and warm for 3-5 minutes.
  4. To make the guacamole, mash the avocado with the sour cream, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. To assemble the tacos, spread each flour tortilla with 2 tablespoons of refried beans. Wrap the tortilla gently around a crisp taco shell. Top with 2 tablespoons of ground beef, 2 tablespoons of Cheddar cheese, shredded lettuce, tomato, onion, sour cream, and guacamole.
